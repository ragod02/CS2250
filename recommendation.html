  • Jujutsu Kaisen is a great show with great anime and plot. As the viewer we dive into the story of Yuji Itadori as we experience the world of curses along side him. The show has a great cast of characters, which all have their time to shine as if they were the main characters too. Season 3 is set for this January and I am excited to see what it can offer.
  • Konosuba is one of my favorite reincarnation stories because of the mix of high fantasy and commedy. Originally I went into expecting this to be more of a serious story, but came out pleasently surprised. Besides the commedy the story is great too as we follow Kazuma, where he gets through deadly encounters despite being weak. With high quality animation and story, it only makes sense how it has a movie, a spin off show, 4 OVA's, and 4th season coming out.
  • I had no clue what I was getting myself into when watching Erased, but I am happy that I watched it. The plot of going into the past of solving your childhood friend's murder kept me engaged. The twist near the end was also great and shocked me. I felt like I was solving the mystery along side with the main character, Satoru. A sad but good story for those who want a mystery show.

  • Magi has to be one of my favorite pieces of fiction. I started with the Anime, but after the shows has been canceled I moved to reading the manga. I loved the hand drawn art better than the anime and just how they show the scale of the magic as well. The story starts as a magical adventure, but later dives into topics like self worth. Would recommend to those who enjoy magic.
  • Berserk's story has been popular for decades, and I am here to continue the praise it deserves. I went into Berserk thinking there was going to be a lot of fighting, but it is much more than just fighting. It is a story of a man who grows up in a hostile environment, which sets him on a path of a mercenary, but then one day changed. He made friends, he met the love of his life, and he met the man who respects the most. He changed and his heart warmed, despite the many battles he faced. All of that changed once the man he respected the most took everything from him and now he is on a quest of revenge. This story is very dark and is not recommended to those who can't handle dark themes, but it is truely a great story.
  • Choujin X is my recommendations for people who getting into reading manga. This is a relativley new manga and has an interesting story. We follow Tokio who is a young man living in an alternate world where there are beings called Choujin. These are beings who have powers, but still look like human. There are a force of Choujin who protect citizens from the bad Choujin, and our character is the main target for these bad Choujin. I think it is a great story, and probably not that hard to get into considering how young it is.

  • Baldur's Gate 3 is 100% my favorite game right now. I have dedicated 100's of hours into it, and I technically only beat the game twice. The game is addicting because of the amount of freedom you have to creating your characters, making descisions, and the amount locations you can visit. The replayabilty is through the roof and you can make multiple playthroughs and find something new most likely. The mechanics can be a bit daunting to those who are not used to the top down view, but I recommend the struggle as it will be worth it as you get a hang of it.
  • Elden Ring used to be my favorite game, but definetly my top 3 of all time. This game plays differently from Baldur's Gate as you are controlling a character in a 3rd person view. The gameplay is much more intimate since you are always doing something like attacking or dodging. Don't worry, combat is not restricted as all since you can choose multiple ways to play. You can choose to wield giant weapons to crash on oppoents, swiftly attacking with small weapons multiple times, or even casting spells that have powerful affects. The replayability is high as you can make new characters with different playstyles. I recommend this game to those who don't mind playing a game where they are going to lose a lot.
  • Marvel Rivals is a very different game from the other 2 games, but I wanted recommend to those with different tastes. This is a game where you play as a team of Marvel Superheros, trying to gain control over an objective. The reason I recommend this game is because these characters are recognizable for most people, especially since marvel movies were popular before. This game can be played with 5 other people too, so if you had other friends then there is a lot of space for them to join. The game is only online multiplayer, so it is constantly being updating and gaining new content. For the most part the replayability is good since there are an assortment of characters to choose from and you can get new teams to play against, but you can get burnt if you are constantly playing.
